FinOps Strategy
The Problem
A persistent SageMaker endpoint (ml.m5.xlarge) costs $150–200/month even with zero traffic — called "Zombie Infrastructure."
The Solution
✓ Serverless Inference — ~$0.00002/sec, zero idle cost
✓ Auto-Shutdown Script — Boto3 checks traffic hourly
✓ CloudWatch Alarm — Billing alert at $5.00
✓ GPU Training Jobs — 10-min burst then auto-terminates
